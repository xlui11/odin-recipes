Burger Recipe

Description

Here’s a perfect recipe for a Truffle Smash Burger—a luxurious and flavorful twist on the classic smash burger.
The key here is the truffle oil and some quality ingredients to elevate the flavors.

Truffle Smash Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (80/20 ratio for juiciness)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper (for seasoning)
  • 4 burger buns (preferably brioche or potato buns)
  • tbsp truffle oil (white or black, depending on your preference)
  • 1 tbsp truffle butter (optional but adds richness)
  • 1 cup arugula or mixed greens (optional, but adds a fresh contrast)
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced and caramelized
  • 4 slices of your favorite cheese (preferably Gruyère or aged cheddar)
  • Truffle salt (for extra flavor, optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. caramelize the onions: In a pan, heat butter and olive oil over medium heat. Add thinly sliced onions, sprinkle with salt and sugar, and cook for 20-30 minutes until golden brown and caramelized. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the beef patties: Divide 1 lb of ground beef into 4 balls (about 4 oz each). Roll into balls and set aside.
  3. Smash the patties: Heat a griddle or skillet over medium-high heat, drizzle with truffle oil. Place a beef ball on the hot surface and smash it flat with a spatula. Season with salt and pepper, then cook for 2-3 minutes until a crispy crust forms.
  4. Flip and add cheese: Flip the patty, add a slice of cheese, and cover to melt for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Toast the buns: While patties cook, slice buns and toast them with butter or truffle butter in a separate pan until golden.
  6. Assemble the burgers: Brush the toasted buns with truffle oil, then place arugula or greens on the bottom bun. Add the cheese-topped patty, caramelized onions, and any extra truffle oil or truffle salt.
  7. Serve: Top with the other bun half and serve immediately with fries or truffle fries.

Enjoy your indulgent Truffle Smash Burger!
